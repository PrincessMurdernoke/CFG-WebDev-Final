The Folktale

The history of the British Isles is so often recorded in battles between men, fighting for things like land, control, religion and power, paying little to no attention to the tales of things more ancient than the idea of ownership, more enticing than the brightest of jewels and more dangerous than the sharpest of swords

It is in these ancient tales that we find the true history of these lands, from the sirens of the seas, to the landlocked mermaids of the peaks and the horned horses of the north

We invite you to discover the tales which may someday be lost to the sands of time, only through storytelling will we be able to keep them alive and prevent ourselves from falling foul of things we may truly, never understand
